Chicken Parmesan served with Spaghetti

Chicken Parmesan or Chicken Parmesan is an Italian-American dish made from breaded chicken breast that can be baked, pan fried or deep fried covered with mozzarella cheese and tomato sauce, that can be accompanied with several dishes such as bread and pasta. For this dish we will serve it alongside Spaghetti pasta.

Ingredients Needed (Measurements listed are for four servings):

Tomato Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 medium garlic cloves, peeled and crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • ½ teaspoon dried basil
  • ¼ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon sugar
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Pasta:

  • 8 ounces spaghetti or linguine

Breaded Chicken Breasts:

  • 2 large boneless, skinless chicken breasts (6 to 8 ounces each), halved crosswise
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ cup dry bread crumbs
  • freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • ½ cup grated part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese, plus more for serving

Method:

  1. First create the tomato sauce: In a pan, heat oil and garlic over medium to high heat until the garlic starts to sizzle.
    After, stir in tomatoes, basil, oregano and sugar, seasoning it with salt and pepper. Bring the pan to a simmer and cook the sauce slightly thickens. This typically takes 10 to 12 minutes
    Then cover, keep warm and set aside. (Note: the sauce made will be good for two servings so set aside half for another time.)
  2. Next, to prepare the pasta, boil a large pot of salted water and place the spaghetti pasta inside, cooking it until al dente, or tender but firm when bitten. For spaghetti pasta this will usually take 6 to 8 minutes.
  3. To prepare the chicken, lay the chicken breasts between 2 plastic sheets and pound the chicken, using either a mallet or a pan until ¼ inches thick.
  4. After prepare the egg wash and breadcrumbs to bread the chicken with by beating an egg inside a shallow dish or small bowl unil well blended. In a separate plate or tray, season your breadcrumbs with black pepper.
  5. Preheat oven broiler to high and place a rack 4 to 5 inches from the heat source
  6. Dip each chicken cutlet in the beaten egg and then the breadcrumbs, lay the breaded cutlets in a clean wire rack over a cookie sheet.
  7. Heat ¼ cup of oil in a 12-inch skillet over medium high heat until oil shimmers.
  8. Fry the cutlets inside the skillet until golden brown each side, typically about 5 minutes, then place the fried cutlets on the clean wire rack with the cookie sheet.
  9. Top each cutlet with mozzarella and parmesan cheese and place inside the oven, broil until the cheese melts and becomes spotty brown, typically 2-3 minutes.
  10. Portion pasta into 4 plates and add as much sauce as desired per person, add one chicken cutlet on top for each plate, optionally serve with extra parmesan.

Main Characters:

  • Joel Miller: A father turned black market smuggler in the Boston Quarantine Zone after the death of his daughter Sarah who becomes tasked to protect 14 year old girl Ellie Williams.
  • Ellie Williams: A 14 year old orphan who has shown to be immune to the Cordyceps brain infection that ravaged the United States and is believed to be the key to creating a vaccine against the Cordyceps infection.

Reoccuring Characters (Season 1):

  • Sarah Miller: Joel's 14 year old daughter who dies in the early days of the outbreak
  • Tommy Miller: Joel's younger brother and a former Firefly member who eventually settles down in Jackson, Wyoming with his wife Maria
  • Marlene: The leader of a resistance movement named the Fireflies
  • Tess: Joel's partner who accompanies him in his mission to escort Ellie
  • Henry Burrell and Sam Burrell: Two brothers who Ellie and Joel encounter who are on the run from revolutionaries in Kansas City
  • David: A preacher and leader of a community that turns to cannibalism to survive
  • James: David's right hand man

Characters announced for Season 2

  • Abby Anderson: A soldier who seeks vengeance for a loved one and subsequently has her worldview challenged
  • Jesse: An important member of his community whose selflessness sometimes comes at a cost
  • Dina: Ellie's romantic interest and Jesse's ex. She is a freewheeling spirit with a loyalty towards Ellie, which is challenged by the world's brutality
  • Manny: A loyal soldier who fears failing his friends. He maintains a jovial attitude despite the pain of his past
  • Mel: A doctor committed to her role while struggling with the realities of war
  • Nora: A military medic who has difficulty accepting her past behavior
  • Gail: Joel's Therapist
  • Owen: A gentle person whose physical strength forces him to fight enemies he does not hate
  • Issac Dixon: The leader of a militia who faces an ongoing war in their pursuit for liberty
